Problem

Lower-body injuries are common, expensive, and often detected too late for athletes outside elite programs.

Solution

ATHLETIQ turns standard video and wearable signals into biomechanical risk scores, coach-facing alerts, and plain-English guidance.

Why now

Computer vision is finally cheap enough to scale, and DSU's patent gives your team a differentiated scientific layer beyond pose detection alone.

System overview

Four layers of the ATHLETIQ platform

This gives you a clean story on stage: capture the athlete, apply the patent, generate a digital twin, and deliver decisions through the backend dashboard.

Layer 1

Capture

  • Apple Vision Pro body tracking
  • Apple Watch IMU + HealthKit
  • YOLO pose fallback from standard camera video

Layer 2

DSU Patent Engine

  • Muscle activity correlation
  • Ground reaction force and ankle torque vectors
  • Joint displacement and stability score

Layer 3

AI Athlete Digital Twin

  • Personalized baseline model
  • Fatigue pattern learning
  • Predictive what-if simulations

Layer 4

Backend Intelligence

  • FastAPI endpoints and profile store
  • Baseline divergence engine
  • AI coach integration for plain-English insight
